Devenshire Lending

Devonshire Lending is a strong regional savings and lending company that has had local success with a number of niche lending products. Our market is primarily affluent and well-traveled community of Devonshire.

Our Aim

At Devonshire, we aim at- Increasing internal efficiency by automating the customer facing processes that are currently manual[AS1].
Reducing the overhead by reducing the number of brick and mortar branches

We are Growing

While we currently have a strong foothold in the United States, we are looking forward to expanding that market offshores to Canada, and Mexico. OBS system can handle regional differences. Additionally, as we expand into Canada and Mexico OBS will to offering services in Spanish and French.

We are Ubiquitous

Tech savvy customers today require ubiquitous access to their accounts. Today mobile applications are essential (both Android and IPhone) as well as browser-based access from any computing platform. Our focus is on low cost convenience (reduce fees for services and convenient means for accessing accounts and services)
